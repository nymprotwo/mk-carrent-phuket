FAQ

Common questions

What documents do I need to rent a car? +

You need a valid driver's license and a passport. An international driving permit is recommended but often not strictly required for short trips. We'll confirm the requirements when you book.

Do you deliver to the airport? +

Yes — we deliver to Phuket International Airport and any hotel or villa on the island. Just tell us your location and preferred time when booking.

Is insurance included in the price? +

Yes. All rentals include full third-party insurance. The price we quote is the final price — no extra charges for insurance, delivery, or fuel (car is delivered full).

Do you offer long-term discounts? +

Yes — for rentals of 7 days or more we offer reduced rates. Contact us on WhatsApp with your dates and we'll give you the best price available.
